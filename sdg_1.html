The 17 Sustainable Goals

What

Sustainable development goals are an agenda adopted by the general assembly of the United Nations in the year 2015. It consists of 17 goals and emphasizes holistic approaches to sustainable development. It is a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all". They address the global challenges we face today, the global issues like climate change, poverty, hunger and many other more. They are the ultimate goals that are integral to be achieved in order to create a sustainable future.
