Goal 1 - No Poverty

The First goal is to end poverty in all its forms everywhere by 2030.

Poverty, one of the most greatest challenges which more than 700 million people face world wide. Almost 10 percent of the world population, still live in extreme poverty today, struggling to fulfil the most basic needs like health, education, and access to water and sanitation.
- According to globalissues.org, at least 80% of the world's population survives on less than $10 a day, which results in the deaths of 1.5 million people annually, more than half of whom are children under the age of five, that is 4000 deaths every day.
- According to UNICEF, 22,000 children die every day as a result of poverty, and 1 billion of them, which is half the children populatiopn, suffering from poverty 

Solution

There are numerous ways we can achieve this goal and fight against this issue. These include-
1. Donation to humanitarian organisations and activities
2.Education to children
3.Raising awareness on this issue
4.Promotion of quality education, and better facilities /p>
